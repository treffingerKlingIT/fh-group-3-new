WSWED (Wann sind wir endlich da?) Run Club

MEMBERS

Description

We aren't out here hunting Olympic medals. We’re a group of friends who realized that moving your legs is a lot easier when you’re laughing. Our philosophy is simple: No one gets left behind. Every route is meticulously planned to be accessible, scenic, and—most importantly—easy for everyone to crush. Whether we’re doing a casual 5k or a costume-clad themed run, the goal is always vibes over velocity.
